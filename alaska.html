MAY 30ANIMALS & EDUCATION
🕒 Leave: 9:30 - 9:45 AM 📍 Reach: 10:00 - 10:15 AM

Zoo, History & Chocolate Waterfalls

🥦 Food Note: Pack snacks for the zoo; return home for lunch before the trolley tour.
  • Alaska Zoo: Very close to Airbnb; stroller-friendly and great for the kids.
  • Discovery Center: Interactive science exhibits and "Chomper's Corner" snapping turtle.
  • Trolley Tour: Narrated tour of Earthquake Park and Lake Hood.
  • Aayu's Note: Visit **Alaska Wild Berry Products** to see the massive chocolate waterfall!

MAY 31DENALI FLIGHTSEEING
🕒 Leave: 7:00 - 7:30 AM 📍 Reach: 10:00 - 10:15 AM

The Summit Flight & Glacier Landing

🥦 Food Note: Cook and carry lunch.
  • Sunglasses: Essential—the glacier glare is intense from the air and on the ice.
  • Aayu's Note: Wear layers; the glacier landing spot is 10-15°F colder than the airport.
  • Logistics: Check-in 45 mins early at the octagonal log building at Talkeetna Airport.

JUNE 1SCENIC SOUTH DRIVE

Coastal Road & Native Wildlife

  • AWCC: Drive-through enclosures for bears, moose, and bison.
