ALASKA EXPEDITION 2026

May 28 – June 5

🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

MAY 28ARRIVAL & SETTLING IN

Touchdown in Anchorage

  • Land at ANC Airport and pick up 2 Compact AWD SUVs from Enterprise.
  • Drive 7-8 miles to the Airbnb: 3250 Racquet Circle, Anchorage, AK 99507.
  • Grocery Run: Head to the Costco (just 3 miles away) to stock up on vegetarian essentials, snacks, and supplies for the driving days ahead.
🥦 Dining Note: With a fully stocked Airbnb kitchen, tonight is a great night for a relaxed, home-cooked family dinner after a long travel day.
MAY 29THE ICE DAY

Matanuska Glacier (Antelope Canyon with Ice)

Matanuska Glacier ice trek
  • 100-mile scenic drive Northeast on the Glenn Highway.
  • Activity: We will utilize the "Divide & Conquer" strategy here.
    - Trekkers (Alok, Aayushi, Niyati): Will take a guided tour to walk on the deep blue ice and crevasses.
    - Scenic Explorers (Umesh, Seema, Ananya, Amaira, Ariha): Will enjoy the easy "Toe" walk at the Recreation Site and wildlife spotting at Sheep Mountain Lodge.
MAY 30ANIMALS & EDUCATION

Alaska Zoo & City History

Alaska Zoo Anchorage
  • Morning: Visit the Alaska Zoo (only 5-6 miles from the Airbnb). A perfect, stroller-friendly pace for Amaira and Ariha.
  • Afternoon: Board the Alaska Trolley for a narrated tour of the city and its history.
MAY 31THE SKY SUMMIT

Denali Flightseeing & Glacier Landing

Ruth Glacier Denali ski plane landing
  • Logistics: 2.5-hour scenic drive north to Talkeetna (14212 E 2nd St). Arrive 45 mins prior to departure.
  • Highlight: 90-minute bush plane flight around Denali’s summit, ending with a ski-plane landing directly on the ice.
  • The Team: 5 Adults, 3 Children (Traveler: Niyati Thosani).
Booking Reference: GYGRFQXFBFQ7
JUNE 1THE SCENIC SOUTH DRIVE

Turnagain Arm & Wildlife

Turnagain Arm Seward Highway
  • The Drive: 1-hour spectacular coastal drive South along the Turnagain Arm. Stop at Beluga Point for views.
  • Alyeska Aerial Tram: Ascend the mountain to view 7 hanging glaciers.
  • AWCC: Drive the SUVs through the large enclosures at the Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center to see bears, moose, and bison.
  • Note: Head back to the Airbnb early to rest up for tomorrow's early train!
JUNE 2THE RAIL DAY

Coastal Classic to Seward

Coastal Classic Alaska Railroad
  • 6:45 AM Start: Board the Coastal Classic Route (Booking #WRR772662).
  • Travel through the roadless wilderness of the Kenai Mountains.
  • Afternoon: Check-in to Seward accommodation and visit the Alaska SeaLife Center.
JUNE 3THE MARINE DAY

Kenai Fjords National Park

Kenai Fjords tidewater glacier calving
  • Activity: 7.5-hour Glacier & Wildlife Cruise with Major Marine Tours.
  • Observe humpback whales, orcas, and massive tidewater glaciers actively calving into the sea.
JUNE 4THE HARBOR DAY

Exit Glacier Exploration

Exit Glacier Nature Center trail
  • Short drive from Seward to the Kenai Fjords Nature Center.
  • Activity: Walk the accessible trails to the "Edge of the Glacier" to see the ice wall and historical recession markers.
JUNE 5DEPARTURE

Return to Anchorage

  • Transfer: ACT BIG BUS narrated transfer departs 10:30 AM from the Seward Cruise Terminal (Confirmation: #144487).
  • Final Drop: Arrive at ANC Airport ~1:00 PM for flights back home.
